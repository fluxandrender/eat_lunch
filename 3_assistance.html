SNAP, TANF, and FDPIR are federal and state assistance programs.

What does this mean?    

Supplemental Nutritional Assistance Program (SNAP) offers nutritional assistance to low income families. For more info, visit: www.snap.com

Temporary Assistance for Needy Families (TANF) provides state-funded financial support for families. For more info visit: www.tanf.com

Food Distribution Program on Indian Reservations (FDPIR) provides food to low-income households that contain at least one federally-recognized tribe member. For more info visit: www.fdpir.com

Call 1-800-[DISTRICT SUPPORT OFFICE #] for more help.
